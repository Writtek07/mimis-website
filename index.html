About Us

Welcome to Mimis Kids School, where we are committed to providing a safe and nurturing environment for students to learn, grow, and thrive. Our school has a long-standing tradition of academic excellence, and we are proud of the achievements of our students and alumni.

  • Our faculty and staff are dedicated professionals who bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to their work. They are committed to helping every student reach their full potential and providing personalized attention and support.

  • At Mimis School, we believe that education is not just about the acquisition of knowledge, but also about the development of character and values. We encourage our students to be responsible, respectful, and compassionate individuals who contribute positively to their communities.

  • Our school offers a range of academic and extracurricular programs designed to meet the needs and interests of our diverse student body. We believe in providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in college and in life.

  • We welcome students from all backgrounds and are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive community. Our school values diversity and strives to promote understanding and respect for all.

Play Group

Our Playgroup program is specially designed for young children aged 2-3 years. It focuses on play-based learning, socialization, and early language and literacy skills. Our experienced teachers provide a nurturing and supportive environment that helps children develop their cognitive, emotional, and physical abilities.
